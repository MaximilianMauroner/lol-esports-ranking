# 2026-06-27 Icon Review

Generated with the Codex `imagegen` skill. These are original app-icon candidates for the LoL Esports Ranking workbench and are not Riot, LoL Esports, team, tournament, or data-source assets.

## Candidates

1. `calibrated-rift-compass.png` - compact compass/radar mark for calibrated ranking and regional strength.
2. `league-strength-matrix.png` - data matrix with rising bars and source/network nodes.
3. `global-power-sigil.png` - shield/sigil with an upward ranking path and global-region arcs.

## Review Scores

| Lens | Winner | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 32px clarity | `global-power-sigil.png` | Shield and rising path read most clearly at favicon size. |
| Product/UI fit | `calibrated-rift-compass.png` | Best match for the dark, technical, cyan-led workbench UI. |
| Brand risk and originality | `calibrated-rift-compass.png` | Reads as an independent analytics/navigation tool rather than an official esports badge. |

## Decision

Selected `calibrated-rift-compass.png` as the active app icon. It wins two of three review lenses and is the best long-term product mark. `global-power-sigil.png` is more legible at 32px, but its shield and arrow language is more generic and closer to official-looking esports branding. `league-strength-matrix.png` is the safest analytics motif, but it loses too much detail at small sizes.
