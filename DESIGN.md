# Design Context

## Visual Register

Product UI. Dense, restrained, table-first, and built for repeated comparison.

## Scene

An esports analyst is comparing teams on a desktop monitor during a broadcast day in a dim room, with frequent filter changes and quick cross-checks between teams, events, and players.

## Palette

- Page: `oklch(18% 0.012 55)`
- Surface: `oklch(22% 0.012 55)`
- Raised surface: `oklch(26% 0.014 55)`
- Border: `oklch(34% 0.014 55)`
- Text: `oklch(91% 0.01 80)`
- Muted text: `oklch(70% 0.012 80)`
- Accent: `oklch(69% 0.13 45)`
- Positive: `oklch(70% 0.13 150)`
- Negative: `oklch(66% 0.16 28)`
- Info: `oklch(72% 0.09 220)`

## Component Rules

- Use tables and split panels as the primary structure.
- Use cards only for repeated summary objects and comparison rows.
- Keep border radius at `8px` or below.
- Use icons for compact controls where familiar.
- Avoid gradients, glass effects, glow effects, and hero sections.
